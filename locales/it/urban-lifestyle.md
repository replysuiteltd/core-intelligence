# Italian Urban Lifestyle & Slang (2026)

- Figo/Figa: Cool, awesome (“È una promo figa!”)
- Top: Excellent, very good (influencer/Gen Z-slang, “Offerta top”) 
- Dm preso: “Direct received”, for chat commerce
- Bloccato: Out of stock (“Questo prodotto è bloccato”)
- Sballo: “Exciting/buzz”, for viral sales
- Farci una promo: Organize/offer a last-minute deal
- Cliente seriale: Repeat buyer—expect to be pampered

__Social:
Mobile chat for everything—deals, receipts, “arrivederci” at the close.
