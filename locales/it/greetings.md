# Italian Business Greetings & Openers

- Buongiorno: Good morning (formal, business hours)
- Buonasera: Good evening (after 5pm, formal)
- Ciao: Casual, friends/peers or youth in chat
- Salve: Neutral, safe for phone or email
- Come posso aiutarla?: How may I help you? (formal)
- Tutto bene?: How’s everything? (rapport)

__Etiquette__: Use first name only if client signs off with it or chat is informal.
