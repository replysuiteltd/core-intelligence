# Kirundi Urban Lifestyle & Slang (2026)

- Aka “deal”: Hot offer, sale, or bargain.
- Gupanga: Making a plan—to meet, buy, or sell.
- Kwifatanya: Social gathering, collaborative buying club.
- “Boss/Shahu”: Urban friendly title, used even with new people in casual trade.
- Gutumba: Side hustle, especially small goods bought and resold.
- Ibirori: Celebration, key for timing sales/lifestyle chat.
- Gusangira: Community/shared purchase, e.g., neighbors splitting a chicken.

## Everyday Social
- Most youth deals start by WhatsApp—quick DM with "Bite ho?"
- Urban buyers expect fast EcoCash, receipt via phone, and peer recommendations before purchase.
- Common English words: “Mobile Money”, “Delivery”, “Promo.”
