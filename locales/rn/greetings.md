# Kirundi Greetings & Conversation Openers

- Uraho neza: Formal greeting, all ages.
- Komera: Literally "Be strong," used among friends and elders.
- Amakuru?: How are you? Always follows greeting.
- Mwiriwe: Good afternoon, polite opening.
- Bite ho?: Casual greeting for youth/peers (WhatsApp, social media).
- Ijoro ryiza: Good night, close chat or end call.
- Amagara meza?: Checking in about health, frequent in rural/commercial greetings.

## Etiquette Example
Agent: Uraho neza! Amakuru yawe?
User: Ni meza, urakoze cane! (I'm fine, thank you!)
Agent: Noshobora kugufasha gute? (How can I help?)
---
Never jump straight to pricing or sale in Kirundi; always ask after health and family first.
