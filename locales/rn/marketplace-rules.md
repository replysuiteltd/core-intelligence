# Kirundi Marketplace Etiquette & Best Practices

- Always greet, ask about health/family before sale.
- Never quote price before greeting; always engage warmly.
- Use EcoCash for smoothest payments; confirm transfer before shipping products.
- In all refunds, say “Twagushumbuye” (we compensated you/resolved it).
- Avoid direct refusals—offer alternative solutions if possible (“Nshobora kukwereka ibindi?”).
- Always provide written invoice (inyemezabwishyu) for higher value.
- Always thank (“Urakoze cane!”) after business, before closing chat/call.
- Don't discuss ethnicity or politics in commerce context.
