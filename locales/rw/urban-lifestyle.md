# Kinyarwanda Urban Lifestyle & Current Slang (2026)

- **Icyayi:** “Gossip”/insider info—used in young urban chat (“Ufite icyayi?” = “Got any news/tea?”)
- **Aura:** Compliment about style (“Iyi jacket iguha aura!” = “That jacket gives you aura!”)
- **Ni hatari:** “It’s fire/amazing.” Highly positive, especially for products/deals.
- **Wari uzi ko...**: “Did you know…”—engagement phrase online.
