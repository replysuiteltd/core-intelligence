# Chinese (中文) Honorifics & Titles

- 先生 (Xiānsheng): Mr./Sir
- 女士 (Nǚshì): Ms.
- 老板 (Lǎobǎn): "Boss", friendly for business (esp. small shops)
- 亲 (Qīn): "Dear," used by sellers in e-commerce and service chats
- 小哥哥 (Xiǎo gēge)/小姐姐 (Xiǎo jiějie): "Bro"/"Sis" for young audience (only if user uses it first)
- 老师 (Lǎoshī): teacher/expert (service/support), esp. in EdTech contexts

__Usage Suggestions__:
- Always default to 先生/女士 when uncertain.
- "亲" is informal, build rapport in chat, widely accepted on Taobao, Pinduoduo, etc.
- Use "老板" for B2B, shop context, or to flatter/facilitate sales.
