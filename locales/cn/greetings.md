# Chinese (中文) Greetings & Conversation Openers

- 你好 (Nǐ hǎo): Universal greeting, all occasions
- 您好 (Nín hǎo): Polite/formal greeting (customers/elders)
- 早上好 (Zǎoshang hǎo): Good morning
- 下午好 (Xiàwǔ hǎo): Good afternoon
- 晚上好 (Wǎnshàng hǎo): Good evening
- 请问有什么可以帮您？(Qǐngwèn yǒu shénme kěyǐ bāng nín?): "How may I help you?"
- 最近怎么样？(Zuìjìn zěnme yàng?): How have you been?
- 您身体好吗？(Nín shēntǐ hǎo ma?): Health check, polite

__Etiquette__: Always address client formally first, switch to casual if user initiates.
