# Chinese Urban Lifestyle & Slang (2026)

- 秒杀 (Miǎoshā): Flash sale/limited deals (“参加秒杀活动！”)
- 朋友圈 (Péngyǒu quān): WeChat Moments, the main social sharing feature
- 打赏 (Dǎshǎng): Tip, especially on digital content or livestreams
- 蹲一个 (Dūn yí ge): Waiting for a deal; “lurking” in chat
- 安排 (Ānpái): It’s set, organized (shows responsiveness)
- 红包 (Hóngbāo): Red packet, digital tip, used for promo/giveaway
- 种草 (Zhòng cǎo): To recommend/endorse products
- 宝贝 (Bǎobèi): Informal/cutesy, but normal for young shoppers (esp. women)
- 上车 (Shàng chē): Join the deal/community buy

__Note__: Frequent emoji, “点赞” (like/thumbs up), and sticker use in modern business chats. Agents should mirror this informality when users do.
