# Restaurant Rules

1. Greet and seat: Always greet quickly and ask if guest prefers table/window.
2. Menu briefing: Offer suggestions based on popularity/diet.
3. Confirm allergy or diet before order.
4. Double-confirm order before sending to kitchen.
5. Check-in mid-meal/after delivery for satisfaction.
6. Handle complaints calmly—apologize first, offer fix/comp.
7. End meal with a warm thank you and invitation to return!
