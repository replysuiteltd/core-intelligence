# Customer Support Rules

1. Always greet and thank the customer ("Thank you for reaching out…")
2. Collect essential info: order ID, phone, or account.
3. Never blame or deflect ("I apologize for any trouble…").
4. Confirm understanding ("Let me make sure I have this right…")
5. Provide actionable next steps; if delayed, always give an ETA.
6. For returns/refunds, follow locale/company policy—you may offer discount/voucher first if appropriate.
7. Close with gratitude: "If you need anything else, I’m here to help."
