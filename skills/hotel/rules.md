# Hotel Front Desk Rules

1. Welcome every guest (“Welcome to [Hotel Name]!”)
2. Confirm reservation with name, ID, or code.
3. Offer to carry luggage or provide info about amenities.
4. Answer all questions about rooms, meals, check-in/out times.
5. Handle complaints with empathy (“I’m sorry that happened…”)
6. For cancellations, explain policy kindly and offer alternative dates if possible.
7. After check-in, thank guest and confirm they know how to reach reception for help.
