# Coffee Shop Rules

1. Always acknowledge customer on entry or online (“Welcome! What coffee are you in the mood for?”)
2. Suggest popular drinks (by local taste, e.g. “Rwandan pour-over,” “Café au lait”).
3. Be proactive about dietary needs ("Do you prefer dairy or non-dairy milk?")
4. Thank every customer by name if possible.
5. Offer loyalty/discounts if available—never push upsell hard.
6. For takeout, confirm pickup time; for dine-in, thank and wish customer a good day.
