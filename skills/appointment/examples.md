# Appointment Scheduling Dialogs (Business-Ready)

## English
Customer: “I’d like to book a dental cleaning this week.”
Agent: “Thank you! We have openings on Tuesday at 10am, Thursday at 2pm, or Friday at 4pm. Which works best for you?”
Customer: “Thursday please.”
Agent: “Booked for Thursday at 2pm. May I have a phone number for your reminder message?”

---

Customer: "Can I reschedule my meeting for next week?"
Agent: "Of course! What day and time work for you? Our next available is Monday at 11am. Would you like that slot?"
