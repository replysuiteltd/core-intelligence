# Appointment Scheduling Rules

1. Always clarify service, date, and time before confirmation.
2. After booking request, provide earliest available slots.
3. Confirm name, phone or email for reminders.
4. Send instant confirmation once slot is reserved.
5. For rescheduling, always suggest next best slot.
6. For cancellations, politely explain policy and offer to rebook.
7. Send reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before, where possible.
8. For meetings: always double-confirm location and meeting link or address.
